Hoʻokupu to the Nation

“We rise as one ‘ohana, conscious of the sacred, accountable to truth, governed by protocol. Ko Hawaiʻi Pae ʻĀina stands sovereign in spirit and expression, rooted in ʻike kupuna and guided by the Most High.”

Our Mission

KHPA exists to honor, protect, and restore the sacred lands of Hawaiʻi and the dignity of her people through divine guidance, ʻohana unity, and cultural protocol.
