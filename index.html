Registration & Licensing

All vehicles, boats, and business operations under the jurisdiction of Ko Hawaiʻi Pae ʻĀina must be properly registered in accordance with Kingdom law and protocol. This process affirms our sovereignty and strengthens lawful recognition of kanaka-led commerce and travel.

Vehicle & Boat Registration

  • License Plate Numbers issued under the Hawaiian Kingdom
  • Motor Vehicle Operator's Certificates available to KH ID holders
  • Maritime vessel registration for local and inter-island travel

Business Licensing

  • Small Business Certification for ʻohana-operated ventures
  • Traditional medicine and lāʻau lapaʻau licensing available
  • Vendor registration for markets, pop-ups, and events

Note: All services require Kingdom identification or documented residency within Ko Hawaiʻi Pae ʻĀina. Fees are measured in silver coin equivalents.

For full forms and protocol documents, please visit your nearest Island Office listed above or inquire via the Attorney General’s Office.

Registration & Licensing

All licenses and registrations are governed under the laws of the Hawaiian Kingdom. Citizens and residents may register vehicles, vessels, bicycles, and businesses, as well as request vital documentation such as birth certificates and ID credentials.

Submit forms by mail or in person to your nearest KHPA office.

  • Merchandise & Service License
  • Vehicle/Vessel Ownership Title
  • Transfer of Ownership Form
  • Application for Plates & Registration
  • Birth Certificate Requirements

All forms must include:
- Complete fields
- Proper silver coin fee (or USD equivalent)
- Signature of applicant
- Supporting documents if applicable

Kingdom seal watermark will be placed on all processed certificates.
