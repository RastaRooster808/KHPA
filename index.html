Proclamation of Kamehameha III

Struggle. Strength. Peace. This sacred declaration affirms the sovereignty of our lands, neutrality among nations, and the protection of our people. The legacy of aloha ʻāina continues as we restore our foundation with purpose and protocol.

Submission Notice

All documents must be submitted in person at your nearest island office. Each location is authorized under the Kingdom of the Hawaiian Islands to verify and accept your forms. Mahalo for respecting the process of sacred governance.
